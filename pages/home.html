Our Story

Faith Baptist Tabernacle was organized on May 11, 1980, when thirty charter members gathered in a small community room in Jamestown with a vision: glorify God by proclaiming the Gospel and building up believers through the teaching of His Word.

Today that vision continues — from Faith Christian Academy (founded 1998) to supporting over 75 missionaries and mission projects around the world.

"Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost." Matthew 28:19
Our Leadership

Pastor Josh Grubbs

Called to Faith Baptist Tabernacle in January 2019 — only the second pastor in the church's history — Pastor Josh desires to lead a ministry that helps people love God supremely, grow together in faith community, and serve others humbly. He and his wife Kim have three adult children, each serving the Lord with their spouses.
